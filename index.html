/***** ==========================================================  *  *  GREG — HOW THIS SYSTEM WORKS  *  *  Greg is an automatic ad safety system.  *  His job is to stop ads that are clearly wasting  ----------------------------------------------------------  *  Greg ONLY looks at:  *   - ACTIVE ads  *   - inside ACTIVE campaigns  *   - where the campaign name includes "b2c"  *  *  Anything else account has an owner (CSM).  *  Greg only runs if that owner is enabled in OWNER_RUN_FLAGS.  *  This lets Greg be ON for some CSMs and OFF for others.  *  *  ----------------------------------------------------------  *  HOW GREG SETS THE LEAD CPL GOAL (DYNAMIC + CLAMPED)  *  ----------------------------------------------------------  *  Each account has = $1,200  *   - Recent leads = 40, booked = 20 → rate = 50%  *   - Lead CPL Goal = $1,200 × 50% = $600  *  *  If there are NOT enough recent leads (ex: under 30),  *  Greg uses a safe default rate (ex: 55%).  *  *  IMPORTANT: The rate is CLAMPED to prevent extremes.  *   - Minimum rate: 37.5%  *   - Maximum rate: 60%  *  *  Example:  *   - Booking CPL Goal = $1,200  *   - Lead CPL Goal will always stay between:  *     $450 (37.5%) and $720 (60%)  *  *  ----------------------------------------------------------  *  THE FIRST GATE: MINIMUM SPEND  *  ----------------------------------------------------------  *  Greg ignores an ad until it spends at least $100.  *  If spend < $100 → Greg does nothing.  *  *  ----------------------------------------------------------  *  BANDS (BASED ON LIFETIME LEADS)  *  ----------------------------------------------------------  *   - 0 leads ever   → ZERO band  *   - 1 lead ever    → ONE band  *   - 2–3 leads ever → LOW band  *   - 4+ leads ever  → PROVEN band  *  *  Once an ad moves UP a band, it never moves back down.  *  *  ----------------------------------------------------------  *  WHEN GREG PAUSES ADS (ALL BASED ON THE LEAD CPL GOAL)  *  ----------------------------------------------------------  *  Greg uses TWO checks:  *   1) Is CPL far above the goal?  *   2) Has the ad spent enough money to prove it?  *  *  Greg also ignores tiny overages:  *   - If CPL is less than $5 over goal, Greg will not pause it. 1.8  *  *  Example (goal $120):  *   - leash = $216  *   - if spend ≥ $216 and leads = 0 → PAUSE  *  *  ----------------------------------------------------------  *  ONE BAND (1 LIFETIME LEAD)  *
